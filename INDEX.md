## 🎓 Key Concepts

### **Trust Score**
- Range: 0-100
- Updates: After each transaction
- Affects: Transaction limits
- Zones: Green (70+), Yellow (40-69), Red (<40)

### **Payment Modes**
- **Normal**: Standard limits apply
- **Emergency**: +25% limit boost for crisis
- **Lend**: Special lending feature
- **Split**: Share cost with multiple people

### **Promise Tokens**
- Unique ID per transaction
- Prevents double-spending
- Works offline
- Expires in 30 days
- Device signature included

### **AI Analysis**
- Checks 8 factors
- Makes transparent decisions
- Explains every decision
- Detects suspicious patterns
- Learns from transactions
