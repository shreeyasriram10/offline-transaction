## 🚀 **How to Use**

### Making a Payment:
1. Click **💳 Quick Payment** button
2. Enter payment details:
   - Your name
   - Receiver name  
   - Amount (₹)
   - Your PIN
3. Choose mode:
   - **Normal** (1x points)
   - **Emergency** (2x points)
   - **Lend** (1.5x points)
4. Click **Process Payment**
5. See congratulations with points earned! 🎉

### Using Emergency Mode:
1. Click **🚨 Emergency** button
2. Modal explains emergency mode
3. Click **Activate Emergency Mode**
4. You're sent to payment with emergency mode pre-selected
5. Make payment and earn 2x points!

### Checking Points:
- Look at top right corner of screen
- See **⭐ XXX** where XXX is your total points
- Star icon pulses smoothly
- Updates instantly after each payment
